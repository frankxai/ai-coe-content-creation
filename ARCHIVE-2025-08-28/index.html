ü™ù Viral Hook Engineering Prompt

Generate 10 viral hooks for [YOUR TOPIC] using these psychological trigger templates:

1. PATTERN INTERRUPT: "Everyone thinks [common belief]. They're dead wrong."
2. AUTHORITY CHALLENGE: "After [experience], I discovered [contrarian truth]"
3. DATA BOMB: "[Shocking statistic] will change how you think about [topic]"

5. FUTURE SHOCK: "In [timeframe], [prediction]. Here's how to prepare."
6. SOCIAL PROOF TWIST: "[Company A] does this. [Company B] does this. You're doing the opposite."
7. INSIDER SECRET: "What [authority figures] know that you don't"
8. FAILURE STORY: "I lost [specific loss] learning this lesson"
9. BEHIND SCENES: "The conversation that happens when [situation]"
10. CONTROVERSY: "[Popular practice] is killing your [desired outcome]"

For each hook:
- Viral score (1-10)
- Psychological trigger used
- Target emotion
- Scroll-stopping power

Rank all hooks by viral potential and select the strongest.
